# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Engineers and ops teams defining process flows, approval chains, and automation pipelines. Used at a desk in focused work sessions. Primary task on any screen: read and edit a workflow graph.

## Product Purpose

Visual workflow editor built on React Flow. Lets users construct, inspect, and reason about process graphs — nodes, edges, gateways, and annotations. Success means the diagram reads instantly and edits feel effortless.

## Brand Personality

Precise, minimal, fast. No chrome for its own sake. Every UI affordance earns its presence by serving the graph.

## Anti-references

Bloated BPMN tooling (Camunda, Bizagi) — too much chrome, too many panels. Candy-colored no-code builders — not serious enough. Figma plugins that feel like toys.

## Design Principles

1. The canvas is the product — UI furniture stays out of the way.
2. Affordances appear when needed, hide when not.
3. Density without clutter — pack information, not decoration.
4. System colors only — no gratuitous gradients or shadows.
5. Keyboard and pointer parity — power users don't reach for the mouse.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA minimum. Keyboard-navigable canvas. System color-mode support (light/dark) already wired.
